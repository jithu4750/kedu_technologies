Who We Are

KEDU, a company based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, is Technology Consultancy Company specializing in the areas of Telecommunication Services, Information Systems Integration and Development, and Security architecture and solutions provider, delivering technology driven business solutions that meet the strategic objectives of our clients. We deliver unmatched business value to customers through a combination of process excellence, quality frameworks and innovative service delivery models. We believe in partnering with our customer to understand their unique needs and use our expertise to meeting them beyond expectation. We strive for excellence in Customer satisfaction.

Established in 2009, KEDU has proudly served its customers in multiple industries such as Manufacturing, Banking, Hospitality, Telecommunications, and other across the United Arab Emirates and Qatar.

KEDU’s experienced management team brings decades of business, technical, and project management expertise to the deployment, and support of cutting edge technology services and solutions.
